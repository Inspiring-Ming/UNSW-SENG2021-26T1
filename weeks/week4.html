Keeping APIs Secure – Why Does It Matter?

Imagine if someone hacked into your favorite app. What kind of data could be exposed? How can developers protect APIs from security risks?

Discussion Prompts:

  • What personal data do your favorite apps store?
  • What could happen if an API was compromised?
  • What security measures should developers implement?
Example: "My banking app stores my account details and transaction history. If hacked, attackers could steal money or identity. Developers should use encryption, OAuth, and rate limiting to protect the API!"
