Your Favorite App – What Makes It Great?

We all have that one app or website we love using. Maybe it's super fast, easy to use, or has awesome features.

Discussion Prompts:

  • Pick an app you love
  • What makes it great? (Speed, design, features?)
  • If you could add one API-powered feature, what would it be?
Example: "I love Spotify for its smart playlists! I'd add an API that syncs my workout playlist with my heart rate—faster heart rate = faster songs!"
